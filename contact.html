MOIN!

I’m a very conceptual thinker. In my designs nothing is left up to chance. I like to dive deeply into topics to really understand them and communicate them effectively. I also like to educate about topics that are somewhat unknown or only communicated superficially.

My designs are always dictated by the concept. Colors, images, fonts, formats and everything else that makes up a finished project is following the greater concept. This conceptual focus delivers incredibly detailed designs that I am passionate about realizing.
